Colorado's Fly Fishing Outfitter

Eagle River Outfitter provides fly fishing trips on Colorado’s best freestone stream and we’re excited to share it with you. With over 25 years of guiding experience in the Vail Valley, let ERO help you learn, improve and perfect your fly fishing capabilities on the one and only Eagle River.

Eagle River Fishing Reports

From our Colorado fly fishing trips to your eyes, keep up to date with the latest happenings on the Central Rockies' finest, the Eagle River. From bugs to water temps, location and flows, get the most precise and up to date record of what’s going down on the Vail Valley’s favorite freestone gem.

With the goal of honesty and accuracy, learn when to fly fish the Eagle River and when not to fish the Eagle River as well as what to use and why. Revel in our victories, take solace in our defeats. Regardless, read ‘em and weep!

About Us
